About us

We are an old fashioned family bakery and eatery that have been around for several generations of Grannies, with each recipe passed on as heirlooms. We are about tradition. We are about bonds. Granny's Pies is not just a business, it's our pride and joy.

Because of the pride that we hold here, oven fresh goods and swift delivery is guaranteed. Come on in or give us a call for a nice afternoon.

Monthly Worker

The reason that we can proudly call ourselves the best in town is thanks to our devoted workers. Granny's Pies is truely blessed with wonderful people that make it what it is today.

To show our appreciation, each month we spotlight someone from the family to let them know that they are truely appreciated. As such, this month we are excited to feature Ms. Yui Furukawa.

Granny

The current owner of Granny's Pies, Mrs. Furukawa (古河), is a 5th generation Granny who has not only upheld traditional recipes but brought forth many modern pastries to the table in order to keep up with changing times.

Since the moment she earned the title of maître pâtissier in 1956, both formally and by the previous owner's judgement, Granny's Pies has seen continued success.
